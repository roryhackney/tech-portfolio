Policies

Not accepting new clients.
Late Cancels and No Shows
Please notify the office within 24 hours of your scheduled appointment time if you need to cancel or reschedule. This helps keep prices low for clients. Otherwise, you will be charged for the full price of your original appointment. Please remember that this is a small, independent business, and your timely arrival or notification helps keep the business running.
Late Arrivals
Please arrive ten minutes before your appointment to allow time to change and prepare for the service. Late clients will receive treatment for the remainder of their session and will need to pay for their originally scheduled time.
Boundaries
The client will always choose how much clothing to leave on and where they feel comfortable being massaged, and this can change at any time during the session. If you need the temperature, music, or anything else adjusted, simply let Rachel know. Inappropriate discussions or interactions will not be tolerated.
Confidentiality
All conversation and health information in and out of the office will be kept confidential, and any documents will be kept in a locked and secured filing system until they are shredded securely.
Products
Lotion used is hypoallergenic and unscented. Sheets are washed after each session.
Prices
Prices are updated every four years, to keep up with rising costs of living and services. Sliding scale and student/senior pricing is available for clients without insurance who can't afford full price.

Thank you for your business!
