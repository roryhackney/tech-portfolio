FAQs

Not accepting new clients.
What should I expect?
Rachel will welcome you in, discuss your needs, and where you are comfortable being massaged. After that, she will leave the room while you undress to your level of comfort and lie on the massage bed under the sheet. She will apply lotion and massage only where you are comfortable. You may choose a music CD or silence. After the session, she will leave so you may dress, and return to discuss future visits, back care, etc. You may pay at the time of service or at a later time; tips are appreciated.
Do I have to undress?
Undress to your level of comfort. Rachel will only massage where you are comfortable.
Does your lotion have scents or allergens?
Lotion used is hypoallergenic and unscented.
